# CAIS Account Deletion Request
Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting:

richardkumbang04@gmail.com or richard@cbinstrument.com

Please include:
* Registered email
* Username
* Reason for deletion (optional)

Deleted data may include:
* User account information
* Attendance records
* Uploaded profile photos
* Location-related attendance data

Some records may be retained temporarily for administrative or legal purposes.

Processing time: 3–7 business days.
